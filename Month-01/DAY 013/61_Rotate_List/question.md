
# LeetCode 61 - Rotate List

## Question

Given the head of a linked list, rotate the list to the right by `k` places.

---

## Example 1

Input:

```text
head = [1,2,3,4,5]
k = 2
```

Output:

```text
[4,5,1,2,3]
```

---

## Example 2

Input:

```text
head = [0,1,2]
k = 4
```

Output:

```text
[2,0,1]
```
